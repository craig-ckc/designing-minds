Our Story 

Where passion for teaching meets a heart for learners. 

Designing Minds was created from a simple idea,  that every parent deserves easy access to quality learning resources that help their children feel confident and prepared.

We design affordable, CAPS-aligned printable tests that make studying stress-free, structured, and engaging. Our resources are crafted by teachers, tested by parents, and loved by learners across South Africa.

Each test is created to build confidence, reduce exam anxiety, and familiarise learners with real classroom-style questions, so they walk into every test ready to succeed.

Meet Amy – Founder of Designing Minds 

A teacher with a vision for stress-free learning. 

Hi, I’m Amy, and I’ve always been passionate about meaningful learning. After teaching in Vietnam and working with online schools in South Africa, I discovered how powerful accessible learning tools can be. I combined my experience in curriculum development and my love for teaching to create Designing Minds, a platform dedicated to helping learners feel confident, prepared, and supported.

What excites me most is seeing how these resources not only improve marks but also reduce stress and give learners belief in their abilities.
We design affordable, CAPS-aligned printable tests that make studying stress-free, structured, and engaging. Our resources are crafted by teachers, tested by parents, and loved by learners across South Africa.
