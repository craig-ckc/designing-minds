Frequently Asked 

Questions 

What grades are your tests available for? 

We currently offer printable CAPS-aligned tests for Grades 3 to 7 , covering all major subjects. Each term, new tests are released so your child can keep practicing with up-to-date material.

Are your tests aligned with the South African CAPS curriculum? 

Yes, every single test is 100% CAPS-aligned and created by qualified teachers. They follow the same structure, layout, and expectations your child will encounter in the classroom.

How do I receive the tests after purchase? 

Once your payment is complete, your tests are instantly available for download in PDF format. You’ll also receive a confirmation email with download links so you can access them anytime.

Do I need an account to buy tests? 

You can check out as a guest, but we recommend creating an account so you can easily re-download your purchases, track new test releases, and manage your subscription if you choose the annual plan.

What’s the difference between the Term Access and the Annual Subscription? 

- Term Access (Essential) : Pay once per term (R350) and get tests for your selected grade and term only.

- Annual Subscription (Premium) : One yearly payment (R1,200) gives you access to all grades, all subjects, all terms , automatically delivered via email each term — with over R200 in savings.

Can I use the same tests for more than one child? 

Yes, once you’ve purchased the tests, you’re free to print and reuse them for your household . If you’re a teacher or tutor, please contact us for multi-user or classroom licensing .

Can I print the tests from my phone or tablet? 

Yes — all tests are PDF files , so you can download and print them from any device with a printer connection.

What subjects are included in each grade? 

Each grade includes all the core CAPS subjects , such as:

- English Home Language

- Afrikaans FAL

- Mathematics

- Natural Sciences

- Social Sciences (Geography & History)

- Life Skills

All subjects, all terms, one easy plan.
